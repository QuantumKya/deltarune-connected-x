## Devlog #1 - 7/13/2026
# A Passion Project

Deltarune Connected is an awesome game; a playground for roleplaying, time-wasting, or simply for chaos. As a fan of Deltarune, After I heard about it, I was really excited to have some crazy fun playing it and taking screenshots of ridiculous scenarios or whatever. So, I looked it up, ready to install it and play...

That's when I learned it was *Roblox*. Aw, damn it.  
Beyond the fact that I personally dislike using Roblox at all, its recent decisions and changes to its platform has made using Roblox even worse. Their age-detection systems and its moderation strategy are beyond horrid, regardless of their motivations and the even more horrid thing they are aiming to prevent. In my opinion, the best way to respond to these problems with Roblox is to simply go off-platform. Sparser moderation is always a risk, but it is preferable to strong yet badly misguided moderation.

This is my mission:  
not to support the company of Roblox nor to give them biological data from people who just want to play a game.
